How to Get Started

Follow these easy steps and start donating in no time.


Create an account!

We need just a few basic personal info such as mobile number, email, name and address so we can pick your donations up and you can get notified when someone receives it.


Pick your choice

Scroll through a list of donation wishlists and pick wich one you would be most intrested to donate to or even choose donating what is in hand by picking one of our many supply boxes based on category.


Almost done!!

Answer few questions providing us with your free time range, whether you would like to make your donations publicly or annonymously and a location, so we can come and pick your donations up.
